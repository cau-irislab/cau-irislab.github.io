date: 2021-09-01

In September 2025, the **Immersive Reality & Integrated Systems (IRIS) Lab** officially opened at the Graduate School of Advanced Imaging Science, Multimedia & Film (GSAIM), Chung-Ang University. 

IRIS Lab conducts research on the convergence of generative AI and reality, with a focus on computer vision, multimodal learning, 3D scene understanding and generation, digital humans, and embodied intelligence.
