# Join Us

Thank you for your interest in **IRIS Lab** at Chung-Ang University.

We are looking for highly motivated students who are passionate about **Generative AI & Reality** and developing **human-centered intelligent systems**.
If you are interested in joining our lab, please follow the application process below.

---

### Application Process

1. **Email Application**
   - Send your **CV** and **academic transcript** to the PI (Hak Gu Kim) at [hakgukim@cau.ac.kr](mailto:hakgukim@cau.ac.kr).

2. **Interview**
   - Selected applicants will be invited for an **online or in-person interview**.

3. **Graduate School Admission**
   - **Important:** Applying to IRIS Lab is independent of the graduate school admission process.
   - Applicants must also apply to and be admitted to the **Graduate School of Advanced Imaging Science, Multimedia & Film (GSAIM)** at Chung-Ang University.

---

### Qualifications

#### Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or a related field
- Programming skills in **Python** and **PyTorch/JAX**
- Background in **linear algebra**, **probability & statistics**, and **optimization**

#### Preferred:
- Experience in machine learning and computer vision
- English communication skills
- Logical thinking and problem-solving skills
